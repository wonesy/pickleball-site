How It Works

Welcome to Bosky Pickle, your friendly neighborhood pickleball league! Unlike other sports leagues, Bosky Pickle puts you in control of choosing when and where you play your matches. Here's how it works:


  1. Joining the League
    • Register an account to join the league and gain access to the siteâ€™s features
    • Registering will place you in a 6-person division
    • The regular season will be 5 weeks long
    • Everyone plays each other over the course of the season

  2. Match Deadlines and Guidelines
    • Each match has a deadline
    • The HOME team is responsible for any court dues if playing at a paid facility
    • Each match consists of 3 games to 21 points
    • The games are win by 2 or first to 25

  3. Reporting Match Results
    • The HOME team is responsible for submitting match outcomes
    • 40 points are awarded if a player wins the match
    • 20 points are awarded if a player wins at least one game
    • 10 points are awarded if a player participates
    • 0 points are awarded if a player forfeits

  4. Tracking Progress and Standings
    • Standings are based on match points
    • Each player also has a Pickleball Rating (PBR) which is the percentage of games won
    • PBR is used as a tiebreaker for match points and will eventually be used to group players into different leagues

  5. Playoffs and Championship
    • Players are seeded based on their regular season performance
    • The top two teams get a bye
    • The league champion gets bragging rights and some type of prize
